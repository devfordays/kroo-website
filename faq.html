Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Kroo take to setup?

There are 3 steps. 1. Syncing your account...takes 30 seconds. 2. Cleaning up your menu...takes a few hours depending on the changes needed. 3. Shipping your signs...takes a few days. All in all, you can be setup in less than a week!

What payment methods does Kroo support?

Apple Pay and Google Pay as well as all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, and Discover) are supported on the Kroo platform.

Do you ever sell my information?

Hell No! At Kroo we believe firmly in data privacy.

Where is Kroo supported?

Kroo is supported at any restaurant using the Square POS though we will be integrating many other POS providers.

I have technical problem, who do I email?

Contact us directly at support@getkroo.com
